4-Day Weekly Workout (≈ 1 hour each)

Structure: 5 min warm-up · 30–35 min strength · 10–15 min incline walking · 10–15 min stretching.

Day 1 · Lower Body Focus
  • Warm-up: brisk walk, leg swings (5 min)
  • Leg Press · 12 reps × 3 sets
  • Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift · 12 reps × 3 sets
  • Reverse Lunges · 12 steps × 2 sets
  • Incline Walk: incline 8–12%, 10–15 min
  • Stretch: hamstrings, quads, calves, hips · 10–15 min
Day 2 · Upper Body Focus
  • Lat Pulldown · 12 × 3
  • Chest Press (machine or dumbbells) · 12 × 3
  • Seated Row · 12 × 3
  • Plank · 30–45 sec × 2
  • Incline Walk · 10–15 min
  • Full-body stretch · 10–15 min
Day 3 · Full Body Mix
  • Goblet Squat · 12 × 3
  • Dumbbell RDL · 12 × 3
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press · 10 × 3
  • Dead Bug · 10 each side × 2
  • Incline Walk + Stretch · same as above
Day 4 · Lower Body & Core
  • Leg Press · 12 × 3
  • Hip Thrust · 12 × 3
  • Back Extension · 12 × 2
  • Russian Twists · 20 reps × 2
  • Incline Walk + Stretch · same as above

Stretching ideas: YouTube “10 min full body stretch MadFit” or “10 minute post workout stretch”.
